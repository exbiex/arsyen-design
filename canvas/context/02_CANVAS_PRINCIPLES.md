# Canvas Principles

1. Canvas is a Narrative Experience Platform.
2. Content, Structure and Presentation are independent.
3. Sections are Semantic Experience Objects.
4. Components are First-Class Objects.
5. Motion is Narrative Behavior.
6. Themes are Visual Personalities.
7. Design Packs are Creative Operating Modes.
8. Assets are globally owned and referenced.
9. AI uses Intent Patches.
10. Vertical narrative is the primary experience model.
11. Controlled variation over design chaos.
12. Branching before large AI changes.
