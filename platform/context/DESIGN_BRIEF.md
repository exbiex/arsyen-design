# ARSYEN DESIGN SYSTEM REIMAGINING

## Context

The current UI implementation exists and is functional.

Do NOT discard it.

Treat the current implementation as a baseline design direction achieving approximately 4/10 of the intended vision.

Your objective is not to redesign from scratch.

Your objective is to evolve the existing design language into a world-class creative operating system experience.

Preserve what works.

Challenge what doesn't.

Push the design to a 10/10 experience.

Think like:

* Apple Human Interface Team
* Arc Browser Design Team
* Linear Design Team
* Framer Design Team
* Awwwards Creative Studio

combined.

---

# Performance Requirement

Motion quality is more important than motion quantity.

Maintain:

Stable framerates

Smoothness

Responsiveness

Battery efficiency

Avoid excessive particle systems.

Avoid expensive effects.

Avoid motion for the sake of motion.

One perfect animation is better than ten mediocre ones.
